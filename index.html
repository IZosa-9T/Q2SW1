Cats & Soup

the best cats

Cats and soup is an idle mobile game where you collect cats that will cook for you. In this game, there are "collections" which is a set of cats for you to collect that will give you certain adavantages. Cats have different skill levels and talents so be sure to place your cat in the correct station. This table will show some of my favorite cats and short descriptions about them!
